Welcome to Santorini online

Rules

1. Blue player starts

2. You have to move before building

2. You can only place building tiers on adjacent tiles to one of your builders

3. You can place a building tier on any building on the board

4. You can only step on a building tie +/- 1 from your current tier

5. Tier 4 buildings are inactive and cannot be stepped on or built on

Win conditions

1. The player have to step on top of a tier 3 building

2. Rival player can't move or build anymore

3. Rival player leaves the game session
